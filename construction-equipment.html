CONSTRUCTION EQUIPMENT

Alfa Industries provides integrated equipment solutions to companies involved in commercial/ residential facilities and infrastructure construction fields in Eastern Europe. Reliability and quality of this heavy industrial technologies is extremely important for any construction project: reputation of US and European made equipment guarantees limited down time in utilization and safety of the operators and overall success of the project. Maintenance and parts provision always accompanies any purchases we service for our clients. ALFA Industries can supply the following categories of construction equipment:

  • Crane and Lift Systems: stationary and mobile, height specific
  • Earthworks: crawlers, dozers, excavators, graders, loaders, forklifts, etc.
  • Scaffolding
  • Roads and Asphalt paving systems, curb and gutter equipment
  • Concrete mixers and pumping delivery systems
  • Concrete plants
  • HVAC, Electrical and security systems
  • Fire safety solutions
  • Site and Employee safety equipment
