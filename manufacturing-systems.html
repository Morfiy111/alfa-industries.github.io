AUTOMATED MANUFACTURING SYSTEMS

As domestic manufacturing of products in Eastern European region has increased significantly over the past years with governmental policies of import substitution, ALFA Industries began to offer its clients complete project assistance with purchasing, installation and implementation of various manufacturing equipment and automated line technologies made in USA. Whether it is industrial or consumer products manufacturing, we can deliver brand new or refurbished production lines and integrated manufacturing facilities/ plants and provide start up and training assistance. This can be done on partial item needs basis or as complete turn key project. The following is a short list of the industries for which we can provide ready and automated production line solutions:

  • Automotive components and assembly
  • Electronics, IT and telecom
  • Construction products and technologies
  • General and specific consumer products manufacturing
  • Medicinal substances and compounding, supplies
  • Food and drink production and processing
  • End-product packaging systems
