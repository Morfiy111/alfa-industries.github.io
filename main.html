COMPANY ALFA INDUSTRIES

ALFA Industries was formed in 2004 with the purpose of providing innovative export/ import commercial services and diversified products to customers and clients in USA and Eastern Europe (primarily countries of former Soviet Union – CIS). Primary core expertise and commercial interests of the Company are in global product sales, distribution, manufacturing and consulting services within the following industries: oil and gas, energy, construction, chemicals, transportation, aerospace, agriculture/ forestry, manufacturing/ automation, waste/ environmental. We have established office and experienced management presence in both USA and Eastern European countries.

Our goal is to assist our clients and partners to achieve success in international markets. Companies are offered guidance to choose the best combination of strategies to suit their own individual requirements. All services are designed to provide our clients with a roadmap to success by implementing strategic planning, understanding needs, developing capabilities, finding opportunities, matching with potential partners, arranging introductions, and most importantly, ensuring continued project support and management.

SERVICES

ALFA Industries is one of the leading providers of comprehensive international project management services to clients with focus on Eastern Europe (CIS) – US based trade ventures. We respond to clients' complex business challenges with a global approach by offering services that span industry sectors and national boundaries. ALFA Industries is dedicated to providing subject matter expertise, human resources and technological tools necessary to support our client’s needs.

Our services are designed to reflect goals and objectives of each client, whether we are dealing with the manufacturing aspects of a cross-border market entry or developing and helping to implement a region-specific product distribution strategy. In practical terms, that means ALFA Industries works with clients to assist them in achieving finAancial results, while helping to control costs.
