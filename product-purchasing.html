PRODUCT & TECHNOLOGIES PURCHASING

For clients who are interested in locating and buying specific products/ technologies and services from USA or Eastern Europe, we offer a fully integrated product sourcing and purchasing service. United States and Eastern Europe are the most diversified and competitive suppliers of manufactured goods and commodities with a staggering number of companies and goods produced in the market place today. ALFA Industries through utilization of its expertise, local offices and long-term personal contacts, can source the products and services client seeks quickly, efficiently and reliably while acquiring them at competitive prices and in strict confidentiality and professional manner.

Our services include:

  • Negotiating the best possible terms and prices with manufacturers
  • Establishment of contractual distributor/ supply arrangements with the manufacturer
  • Supplier due diligence and SWAT analysis
  • Product quality control and compliance with technical specifications
  • Preparing comprehensive quotes including shipping/ logistical costs
  • Handling all local transportation and export/ import documentation
  • Execution of overseas shipping to the most convenient port
