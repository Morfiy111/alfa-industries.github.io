CHEMICALS

ALFA Industries provides our international clients with distribution and purchasing services related to the industry of bulk and specialty chemicals. Our suppliers are located in USA and Eastern Europe and are always vetted on historical performance, reliability, quality, safety and environmental responsibility. Our Company specializes in supplying the following chemical products and compounds:

  • BULK: Urea, Glycerin, Ammonium, Ethanol, Polyol, Methanol
  • Specialty agricultural fertilizers and nutrients
  • Specialty high purity research and manufacturing compounds
  • Pharmaceutical industry chemicals
  • Chemical spill clean-up reagents and kits
