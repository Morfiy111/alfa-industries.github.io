ENERGY SYSTEMS

ALFA Industries specializes in distribution of alternative technologies for generation of energy through utilization of gas microturbines. Continuous world wide drive for liberalization of energy markets has allowed for significant advances in microturbine technology, it also has increased significantly cost effectiveness of such application.

Microturbine technology offers independent and cost-effective solutions for generation of electricity and heat for diversified projects that are geographically located in areas where no traditional energy capacity established or limited. Primarily, ALFA Industries focuses on distribution of microturbines that will be used in Eastern Europe where new manufacturing, governmental and private infrastructure facilities are formed. Key advantages of microturbines include:

  • Power generation capacities: 80 kW, 100 kW, 250 kW.
  • Very low air emissions without use of expensive emission control devices
  • Highly efficient when used in a combined heat and power application
  • Operates on natural and bio (landfill) gas
  • Microturbine technology is proven and well developed
  • Fewer moving parts, relatively low operation and maintenance costs
  • Compact size, light weight, small footprint
  • Highly reliable and durable

We provide full engineering planning, installation and warrantee services for all energy products we offer.
